Meet Our Team

We are a team of six (6) students from Ashesi University, exploring the intersection of differential equations and real-world clinical impact.

Our team brings together students from different majors united by a shared curiosity: what can mathematics tell us about how medicine behaves inside the human body? This project gave us the opportunity to combine rigorous differential equations with a clinical problem that genuinely matters — sepsis treatment in under-resourced healthcare settings across Africa.

Each member contributed to a specific dimension of the project, from mathematical modelling and numerical analysis to simulation, web development, and research writing.
